Target Audience

The MeetConnect Chamber of Commerce website is designed for international business owners seeking community support and insurance to grow their businesses, as well as individuals looking for funding to start or expand their businesses. Additionally, the website is intended for those interested in joining the chamber, with free membership available.

Personas

  1. Karen is a 35-year-old small business owner who runs a hair salon. She wants to join the chamber of commerce to network with other local business owners and gain access to resources that will help her grow her business. She frequently uses her phone to access the internet and is looking for a user-friendly website that provides clear information about the benefits of joining the chamber and how to sign up.
  2. Tom is a 55-year-old retiree who is interested in getting involved in his local community. He is considering joining the chamber of commerce as a way to stay informed about local events and volunteer opportunities. He primarily uses his computer to access the internet and is looking for a website that provides easy access to information about upcoming events, volunteer opportunities, and membership options.

Scenarios

  1. How can I find information about the benefits of joining the meetconnectchamber chamber of commerce?
  2. What resources are available to small business owners through meetconnectchamber chamber of commerce?
  3. How can I sign up to become a member of the meetconnectchamber?
  4. Who can I contact so that I can promote my business as well the upcoming events and volunteer opportunities available through the meetconnectchamber chamber of commerce?
  5. Are there different levels of membership available through the meetconnectchamber chamber of commerce?
